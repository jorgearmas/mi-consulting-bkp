Answers Before You Have to Ask

Common questions

Real questions from manufacturers just like you — answered honestly.

We're a small operation — is SQF certification really worth it for us?
For most small manufacturers, the question isn't whether you can afford to get SQF certified — it's whether you can afford not to. Major retailers, distributors, and co-manufacturing partners increasingly require SQF or an equivalent GFSI-recognized standard as a condition of doing business. Without it, you're locked out of accounts your competitors are winning.
How is MI different from hiring a full-time food safety manager?
A qualified SQF Practitioner costs $70K–$100K+ per year in salary alone — before benefits, turnover risk, or onboarding time. MI gives you that same corporate-level expertise at a fraction of the cost, with no hiring risk and no knowledge walking out the door. And our model develops your existing employees into food safety leaders at the same time — a single hire builds one person's knowledge; MI builds your whole team.
Do we need a food safety program already in place before working with you?
Not at all — that's exactly what Stage 1 (Build) is for. We start from wherever you are. If you have nothing in place, we build your entire SQF program from scratch: HACCP plan, prerequisite programs, SOPs, digital platform, and training. If you have some pieces in place, we assess what's there and fill the gaps.
What happens to our SQF program if we stop working with MI?
It keeps running — because it was built to run without us. From day one, we train your internal people to own the program. Everything lives in your cloud platform, everything is documented in your name, and your team develops the skills to maintain it independently. We're successful when you need us less.
Can you work with us if our floor team primarily speaks Spanish?
Yes — and this is one of the things that makes MI genuinely different. We're fully bilingual in English and Spanish. Your floor team won't just receive translated documents — they'll receive real training, in the language they actually work in, from someone who can answer their questions directly.
Is there a long-term contract?
We work month-to-month. No long-term lock-ins, no cancellation penalties. Our model is built around making you less dependent on us over time — not trapping you in a retainer you can't exit. You stay because the engagement is delivering value, not because a contract says you have to.
